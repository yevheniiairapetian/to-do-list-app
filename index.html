To Do List App

Desktop or laptop devices: Click on "Add New List" to create a topic-related list of To-dos. Double-click to mark an item off, click on "X" to delete an item, and drag items to reorder. If you want to delete all to-dos at once, press CTRL + Left Mouse Click on any to-do or click the trash button. Click the broom button to clean up the input field. Click on the List Title to edit it. Click on Save My Edits Button to save your edits.

Touch screen devices: Tap on "Add New List" to create a topic-related list of To-dos. One tap to mark an item off, click on "X" to delete an item, tap and drag items to reorder. Tap on the List Title to edit it. Click the broom button to clean up the input field.If you want to delete all to-dos at once, tap on the trash button. Tap on Save My Edits Button to save your edits.
