To Do List App

Desktop or laptop devices: enter your next to-do and press "Add". Double-click to mark an item off, click on "X" to delete an item, and drag items to reorder. Click the "Add New List" button to add an editable topic-related list of to-dos.

Touch screen devices: one tap to mark an item off, click on "X" to delete an item, tap and move to reorder. Click the "Add New List" button to add an editable topic-related list of to-dos.
