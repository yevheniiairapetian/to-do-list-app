To Do List App

Desktop or laptop devices: enter your next to-do and press "Add New To-do". Double-click to mark an item off, click on "X" to delete an item, and drag items to reorder. Click the "Add New List" button to add an editable topic-related list of to-dos. If you want to delete all to-dos at once, press CTRL + Left Mouse Click on any to-do or click the trash button. Click the broom button to clean up the input field.

Touch screen devices: enter your next to-do and press "Add New To-do". One tap to mark an item off, click on "X" to delete an item, tap and move to reorder. "Add New List" button to add an editable topic-related list of to-dos. Click the broom button to clean up the input field.If you want to delete all to-dos at once, tap on the trash button.
